DETAIL INFORMATION

Overview

Marvel Studios, a subsidiary of The Walt Disney Company, is a leading American film and television production company renowned for creating the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) films. These films are based on characters from Marvel Comics and are known for their interconnected storylines and high production values.

Key Figures

  • Founded: 1993 (as Marvel Films), rebranded to Marvel Studios in 1996
  • Parent Company: The Walt Disney Company (acquired in 2009)
  • Headquarters: Burbank, California,USA.
  • President: Kevin Feige

Future Projects

  • Thunderbolts (May 2, 2025): A film featuring a team of anti-heroes and villains, directed by Jake Schreier. The cast includes Florence Pugh, Sebastian Stan, and Julia Louis-Dreyfus.
  • Fantastic Four: First Steps (July 25, 2025): Directed by Matt Shakman, this film will feature the MCU debut of the Fantastic Four with a retro future 60s setting.

Production Highlights

Marvel Studios continues to innovate and expand the MCU, integrating beloved characters and introducing new ones. The studio is known for its high production values, interwoven storylines, and post-credit scenes that tease future projects. The studio has a strong presence at major events like San Diego Comic-Con, where they reveal exclusive footage and make significant announcements about upcoming projects.

Milestones

  • First MCU Film: Iron Man (2008)
  • Highest-Grossing Film: Avengers: Endgame (2019)
  • Introduction of Disney+: Expansion into streaming with original series like WandaVision and The Falcon and the Winter Soldier
